How to Install Metamask and Add Bittorent Chain Mainnet

To install Metamask on your browser and add Bittorent Chain mainnet, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the Metamask website (https://metamask.io/) and click on the "Get Chrome Extension" button if you're using Google Chrome or "Get Firefox Extension" if you're using Mozilla Firefox.
  2. Click "Add to Chrome" or "Add to Firefox" and follow the prompts to install Metamask on your browser.
  3. Once installed, click on the Metamask icon in your browser's toolbar and create a new wallet or import an existing one.
  4. Join this website: "medium.com/@BitTorrent/how-to-connect" and follow the instructions to add Bittorent Chain mainnet to your Metamask wallet.
  5. Join this website "bt.io/" and convert your currency to BTT (BitTorrent Token) to save it on Metamask.

Once you have completed these steps, you will be able to send and receive BTT on the Bittorent Chain using your Metamask wallet. Remember to always verify the recipient address before sending any transactions and to keep your seed phrase safe and secure.
